AIVerse's Process of Artificial Intelligence Development

Define

In AI development, the first step is defining the problem by identifying the business issue . Next, gather and prepare accurate, representative data for training the AI model.

MODELING

Selecting the appropriate AI algorithm based on data and training the model with it by feeding data and adjusting parameters for accurate predictions.

Evaluation

Test the AI model's accuracy and performance with a separate dataset to ensure it effectively solves the problem and provides dependable predictions.

Deploy

Put the AI model into real-world use, integrating it with existing systems. Monitor its performance, making adjustments to maintain accuracy and relevance over time.
